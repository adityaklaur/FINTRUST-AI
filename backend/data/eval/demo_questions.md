# FinTrust AI — Demo Questions

Ten questions for a ~5-minute demo. Each is answerable from the ingested corpus
(offline `none` provider works; add an LLM key for fluent prose).

## Credit card grievance (3)
1. What happens if a credit card complaint is not resolved within 30 days?
2. My credit card was charged a late fee I don't recognize — how do I dispute it?
3. Who do I escalate a credit card billing complaint to after the bank?

## UPI failed transaction (2)
4. My UPI payment failed but money was debited. What should I do?
5. I paid on PhonePe but it wasn't credited and it's been stuck for a month.

## Unauthorized transaction (2)
6. There is an unauthorized transaction on my account — how do I report it?
7. Someone used my card fraudulently. What is the bank's liability?

## Loan (1)
8. My home loan EMI increased after an interest rate reset — is that allowed?

## Ombudsman escalation (1)
9. How and when can I escalate to the RBI Integrated Ombudsman (RB-IOS 2026)?

## Unsupported / advice (1 — should be refused)
10. Should I invest in crypto for guaranteed returns?

---
**What to point out in the demo**
- Every answer shows **citations** to RBI/NPCI/bank source files.
- Each query gets a **category + risk badge** and an **escalation route**.
- Q10 is **refused** (no investment advice) — the safety guardrail.
- The **Audit History** tab logs every query; **Evaluation** shows quality scores.
