##### The Logo Size

Usage of UPI HELP in all the partners app, website and communication materials must adhere to the specification provided here and not be tempered with. Sizes given here are for representation purpose but while resizing or using the logo utmost care should be taken to maintain the visibility of the logo.

Digital: In case of the digital communication, the size of the logo should not be less than 40 pixels in width and its corresponding height.

Print: In case of print communication, the size of the logo 
should be minimum 15% of the width of the layout or 
30mm (whichever is higher).

#### UPI HELP FAQs

UPI HELP attempts to resolve UPI transaction related complaints through the UPI payment app

What is UPI HELP?

When amount gets successfully debited from your account and gets successfully credited into receiver’s account, the transaction is successful

What is successful transaction?

When amount gets stuck in payment process either at your end or at receiver’s end, the transaction goes to pending state. Payment confirmation is not received in such scenarios/instances

What is Pending Transaction?

The transaction gets failed when there is some technical issue at your or receiver’s end. You will get your money back in case money is debited

What is Failed Transaction?

Transactions which are done without your permission are termed as fraudulent transactions. Such scenarios 
can happen if UPI PIN is shared with anyone 
Kindly refrain from sharing your UPI PIN 

What is a fraudulent transaction?

• Click on transaction history 
• Select the transaction for raising complaint 
• Click on UPI HELP 
• Select the reason and click on confirm 
• Complaint raised successfully 

How to use UPI HELP?

#### UPI HELP FAQs

You can raise complaint on all debit transactions apart from person-to-person success transactions

What type of transactions are supported under UPI Help?

• Click on transaction history 
• Select the transaction you have raised complaint 
• Click on check complaint status 

How can I know the current status of my complaint?

You can raise complaint after 5 minutes of the transactions

When can I raise complaint on a particular transactions?

No, complaints can only be raised on debit transaction

Can I raise complaint on credit transaction?

Beneficiary/payee/receiver means the person who is going to receive the money

What is the meaning of beneficiary/payee/receiver?

Remitter/payer/sender means the person who is sending the money

What is the meaning of remitter/payer/sender?

Yes, in case of a wrong debit, your money will be credited back in your account. Kindly raise your concern under UPI HELP for easy resolution

Will I get my money back if the transaction is pending/failed?

Refresh button HELPs you to get the present status of transaction. If transaction is in pending state, refresh button HELPs you to get the exact status of transaction
