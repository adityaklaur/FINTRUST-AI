Purpose for FinTrust AI:
Use this for unclaimed deposit search, dormant/unclaimed account guidance, and routing users to the right bank after searching UDGAM.

Key points:

- UDGAM stands for Unclaimed Deposits - Gateway to Access inforMation.
- It lets registered users search unclaimed deposits/accounts across multiple banks in one central place.
- UDGAM does not settle or pay the claim directly.
- The unclaimed deposit can be claimed only from the respective bank.
- Search results may provide an Unclaimed Deposit Reference Number (UDRN) and claim/settlement instructions.
- UDRN is a unique number generated by banks and assigned to each unclaimed account/deposit transferred to the Depositor Education and Awareness (DEA) Fund.
- Deposits remaining unclaimed for 10 years in a bank are transferred to the DEA Fund maintained by RBI.
- Customers/depositors/legal heirs can claim refund from their bank, and the bank then claims reimbursement from the DEA Fund.
