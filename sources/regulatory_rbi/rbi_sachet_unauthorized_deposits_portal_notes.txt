Title: Sachet Portal - Unauthorized Deposits and Fraudulent Schemes
Source URLs:
- https://sachet.rbi.org.in/home

Source type: RBI/Sachet portal research notes

Purpose for FinTrust AI:
Use this for routing complaints about unauthorized deposit schemes, Ponzi/MLM schemes, fake investment schemes, fake employment schemes, fictitious calls/emails, and entities collecting deposits illegally.

Key points:

- Sachet is not the same as RBI CMS Ombudsman.
- Sachet is meant for complaints or information related to deposits/schemes of various companies, unauthorized acceptance of deposits/money, and suspect entities.
- If the complaint is about deficiency in services by RBI-regulated entities such as banks, small finance banks, payment banks, RRBs, cooperative banks, NBFCs, or payment system providers, users should use the RBI Complaint Management System at https://cms.rbi.org.in instead.
- Sachet includes "File a Complaint", "Track your complaint", and "Help your Regulator".
- Complaint categories include fake websites, fictitious phone calls, phishing emails, exorbitant loan interest by registered/unregistered bodies, fraudulent employment schemes, MLM/direct selling/Ponzi schemes, fraudulent collection of fees, unauthorized collection of deposits, and non-repayment of deposits.

Implementation notes:

- Add classifier category: `unauthorized_deposit_or_investment_scheme`.
- If the issue is "bank failed to resolve my service complaint", route to RBI CMS, not Sachet.
- If the issue is "company/app/person collected money illegally or Ponzi/fake investment", route to Sachet.
- Ask for entity name, address/website, amount paid, communication evidence, receipt/agreement, and screenshots.
