Date: 29/02/2024
RBI reference: RBI/DPSS/2023-24/111 CO.DPSS.POLC.No.S1114/02-27-020/2023-2024
Source type: RBI notification clean text extract

Purpose for FinTrust AI:
Use this for BBPS bill-payment complaints, biller/operating-unit responsibility, BBPS reference number, dispute management and grievance redressal.

Key points:

- BBPS is an integrated bill payment platform enabling payment/collection of bills through mobile apps, mobile banking, physical agents, bank branches and multiple payment modes including UPI, internet banking, cards, cash and PPIs.
- NPCI Bharat BillPay Limited (NBBL) is the authorised BBPCU.
- BBPOUs may function as biller operating units (BOU), customer operating units (COU), or both.
- COUs provide customer-facing interfaces for bill payments.
- BOUs onboard billers to BBPS.
- NBBL must provide a framework for redressal of consumer disputes.
- COUs must provide a system for raising disputes.
- All BBPS transactions must have a BBPS reference number from payment initiation stage.
- NBBL must put in place a dispute resolution framework for centralized end-to-end complaint management in compliance with RBI's Online Dispute Resolution (ODR) system for digital payments.
- Participating COUs and BOUs must integrate with the centralized system and enable customers and billers to raise and resolve disputes.
- The BBPS reference number generated while initiating the transaction must be used for complaint/dispute resolution.
- COUs and BOUs must ensure failed transactions are handled according to RBI TAT and compensation instructions for failed transactions.

Implementation notes:

- Add classifier category: `bbps_bill_payment_dispute`.
- Ask for biller name, COU/app/bank used, BBPS reference number, payment date, amount, biller confirmation status, and complaint reference number.
- Link with ODR and TAT sources.
- Do not treat BBPS bill-payment disputes exactly like UPI P2P transfers; use BBPS reference number and biller/COU/BOU concepts.
