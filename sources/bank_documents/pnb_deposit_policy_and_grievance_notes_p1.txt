Key points:
- PNB publishes "Policy of the Bank For Domestic Resident Rupee Deposits" and policy links including KYC-AML Policy, Grievance Redressal Policy, Customer Compensation Policy, Model Policy on Bank Deposits, Cheque Collection Policy and Customer Rights Policy.
- PNB grievance redressal starts with the concerned Branch Head.
- PNB provides online grievance registration and complaint status tracking.
- PNB endeavors to redress complaints within 15 working days.
- For issues needing detailed investigation, PNB sends final response or reasons for further time within 30 days.
- If unresolved within 30 days or unsatisfactory, customer may approach RBI Ombudsman under Integrated Ombudsman Scheme.
- PNB forms page lists nomination forms and deceased account claim forms including cases where nomination is done and not done.
