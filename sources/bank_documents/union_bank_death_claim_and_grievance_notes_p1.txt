Key points:
- Union Bank provides an online Death Claim portal.
- The death claim page links to "Policy On Settlement Of Death Claim 2026-2027" at:
  https://icmt.unionbankofindia.co.in/deathclaim/Documents/2026/Policy%20On%20Settlement%20Of%20Death%20Claim%202026-2027.pdf
- The claim form asks for claimant details, relationship to nominee/legal heirs, mobile, email and security question.
- For complaints related to death claim, Union Bank links to its service portal.
- Contact page provides toll-free numbers and dedicated helplines.
- Fraud/disputed transactions helpline is listed separately.
- Grievance redressal can be initiated through the Union Bank portal.
- Union Bank mentions sign-language services for hearing impaired customers through its website path.
